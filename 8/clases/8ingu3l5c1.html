2️⃣ During: Simple Present Questions 🧠

📓 Notebook work: Please copy this grammar box in your notebook. Use colors if you can!
Does + He / She / It + Verb (base form) + Complement?

Note: The main verb must NOT change because "Does" is already doing the work!

Does Sofía watch her favorite programs?
Does Ricardo ride his bike on weekdays?
