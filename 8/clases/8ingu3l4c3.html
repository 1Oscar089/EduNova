2️⃣ During: My Best Friend's Favorite Free-time Activities 🧑‍🏫

Read the text on the board. Then, mark the statements as True (T) or False (F).

My Best Friend's Favorite Free-time Activities

My best friend's name is Marvin. He is 29 years old and he is a teacher.

In his leisure time, Marvin watches series, does exercise, and plays the guitar.

Marvin doesn't play baseball, but he plays volleyball when he goes to the beach with his friends and pets.

1. Marvin does exercise in his free time.
2. He can play a musical instrument.
3. He plays baseball with his friends and pets.
4. He plays volleyball at the beach.
