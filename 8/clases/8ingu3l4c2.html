2️⃣ During: Negative Statements 🚫

Grammar Rule

To make negative statements with the pronouns he, she, and it, we use the auxiliary verb doesn't (does not). Also, the verb after the auxiliary verb remains in the base form.

Marlene doesn't read comics in the afternoon.
Santiago doesn't play board games with his family.
