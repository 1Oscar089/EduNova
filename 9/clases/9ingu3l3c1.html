1️⃣ Pre: Indefinite Articles Rules! 📖

When do we use "a" or "an"?

We use indefinite articles only with singular countable nouns.

  • We use "a" before words that start with a consonant sound (e.g., a strawberry, a car, a dog).
  • We use "an" before words that start with a vowel sound (e.g., an avocado, an airplane, an elephant).

⚠️ Important! The rule is based on SOUND, not just the letter!

  • an hour (Because the "h" is silent, it starts with an "o" sound).
  • a university (Because it starts with a "y" sound, like "you").
