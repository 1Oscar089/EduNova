2️⃣ During: Spelling Rules 🧠

Rules for Plural Nouns

  • For most nouns, just add -s (apple → apples).
  • Nouns ending in s, x, z, ch, sh: add -es (bus → buses).
  • Nouns ending in consonant + y: change -y to -ies (city → cities).
  • Nouns ending in f or fe: change to -ves (wolf → wolves).
  • Irregular plurals: change the word completely or stay the same (man → men, sheep → sheep).
