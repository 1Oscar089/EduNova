📌 Instructions: This class has 3 phases — 1️⃣ Pre, 2️⃣ During, 3️⃣ Post. Complete each phase to move forward.

1️⃣ Pre: Mystery Box! 🎁

Theory Review

Countable nouns can be counted and have both singular and plural forms (e.g., an apple, two apples).

Uncountable nouns cannot be counted individually and usually only have a singular form (e.g., water, money).

Take one flashcard from the bag/box, show it to your classmates, and say if it represents a countable or uncountable noun. If the noun is countable, you will also have to say its plural form. Finally, write an original sentence using the noun.

3️⃣ Post: Guess the Noun! 🤔

In class, you would elaborate a cardboard headband for each team. You would draw one card from the stack without looking at it so that a partner can stick it on the headband. Then, you ask yes/no questions to try to guess the noun on the card.

Let's play against the computer! The computer has picked a secret card. Ask questions to guess it!
